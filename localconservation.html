Local Conservation

Most people don't think of the importance of environmental conservation in an area that has already been urbanized, especially an area as urbanized as Chicago, however, many plants and animals are able to coexist alongside urban areas such as here in Chicago. To help protect and improve the quality of life of these plants and animals, here are local conservation opportunities we can all participate in!

Here is a list of some websites of local organizations that are currently engaged in local wildlife conservation of some kind or another. Check them out and be sure to check back here occasionally for updates!

  • Chicago Zoological Society
  • Shedd Aquariam
  • Chicago Botanical Gardens
  • Chicagoland Environmental Network
  • Chicago Conservation Corps Blog
  • Chicago Conservation Corps
  • Chicago Audobon Society
  • US Fish and Wildlife Service: Chicago Ecological Services Office
  • Friends of the Chicago River
