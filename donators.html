Donations and Volunteers


We need your HELP!!

ChicagoSafari.com is a non-profit, communtiy run organization and can always use an extra hand, wing, paw, fin, or flipper! To find out how you can best help send us an e-mail!

The best ways of helping us include but are not limited to:

  • Cash donations to us or your favorite eco-charity
  • Volunteer here or at your favorite echo-charity
  • Suggestions and comments on how we can improve
  • Participating in a local conservation project and telling 'em we sent you!
  • Organize an Event in Your Local Community Center
