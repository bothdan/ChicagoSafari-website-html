About Us


 ChicagoSafari.com is an organization that provides both tourists and residents of the city of Chicago a source of information to help us to discover and appreciate the surprising variety of animals and wildlife that carve out a living right alongside us humans in one of the largest cities in the world. Part of our focus is to provide our community with educational opportunities about the importance of conservation along with providing ideas of what we all can do to help protect our magnificent and surprisingly diverse ecosystem.

 ChicagoSafari.com began in the Spring of 2012 as an idea to help associate the city of Chicago with something other than the usual architecture, sports, shopping, Al Capone... We are here as a resource for the community and encourage community participation and feedback to help us maintain the most current and relevant content possible.
