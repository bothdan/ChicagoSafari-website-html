The River

The river has long been a source of ecological debate in the city of Chicago, but with groups such as Friends of the Chicago River working hard for improvements, the river will one day be a thriving ecosystem again. To check out this "diamond in the rough" check out the Friends of the Chicago River website and book a canoe trip or just find out about the latest in Chicago River wildlife news.
